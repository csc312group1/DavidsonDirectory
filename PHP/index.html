How to use online directory

(1) Enter either the first name or the last name for either student, faculty, or staff.
(2) Depending on the query, click "Find Student" or "Find Faculty/Staff" button. Feel free to reset your input using the "Reset" button.
(3) You will be directed to the results page after searching, which has a table of results that match your search request. Select a specific person in mind, and you will be taken to the individual page.
(4) If your search doesn’t yield the expected results, you will be directed to an empty results page. Try searching in a different field.

All information in this directory is drawn directly from Banner; a person’s Banner information may be slightly different from what you expect. Searching for a different type of information may be helpful.

Search keywords are not case sensitive.
