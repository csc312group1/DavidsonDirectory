How to use online directory

(1) Click the button for Faculty/Staff or Student.
(2) Enter all or part of a first name, last name or middle initial to search for faculty, students or staff.
(3) Click on Fac/Staff Advanced Search to search by all or part of a person’s job title or department.
(Example: Mary Smith, Mary, smith, smi, professor, prof, chem)
(4) If your search doesn’t yield the expected results, try searching in a different field.

All information in this directory is drawn directly from Banner; a person’s Banner information may be slightly different from what you expect. Searching for a different type of information may be helpful.

Search keywords are not case sensitive.
